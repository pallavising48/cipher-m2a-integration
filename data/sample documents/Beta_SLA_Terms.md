# GAMMA TECHNOLOGIES — Customer SLA Commitments & Performance Report

**Q4 2025** | Document Date: January 12, 2026 | Prepared by: Engineering

## 1. SLA Commitments to Customers

Beta Technologies commits to the following service levels in customer agreements. Note: Beta SLA terms differ from Alpha Corp's standard SLA framework and will require harmonization post-acquisition.

| SLA Metric | Committed Level | Measurement Window | Penalty if Breached |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platform Uptime | 99.5% | Monthly rolling | 3% credit per 0.5% below threshold |
| API Response Time (P95) | < 500ms | Weekly average | No financial penalty — credit only |
| Support Response — P1 Issues | < 2 hours acknowledgment | Per incident | No financial penalty clause |
| Support Response — P2 Issues | < 8 hours acknowledgment | Per incident | No financial penalty clause |
| Data Availability | 99% monthly | Monthly | 2% credit per 1% below threshold |

**KEY DIFFERENCE FROM DELTA:** Beta's SLA commitments are materially weaker than Alpha's. Alpha commits to 99.9% uptime with cash penalties; Beta commits to 99.5% with credits only. Alpha's P1 response SLA is 1 hour vs Beta's 2 hours. This gap must be addressed before Beta customers are transitioned to Alpha's platform and support model.
